Delicious Recipe

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Dice the ripe avocado into bite-sized chunks. Cut the cherry tomatoes in halves. Chop fresh basil leaves.
  2. Combine: In a bowl, combine the diced avocado and halved cherry tomatoes.
  3. Add Basil: Toss in the chopped fresh basil leaves.
  4. Mix Gently: Gently mix the ingredients to ensure even distribution of flavors.
  5. Serve: Serve the salad immediately as a refreshing and healthy side dish.
